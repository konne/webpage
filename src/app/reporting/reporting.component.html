Do You Want to Do More With Excel?

With Sense Excel REPORTING you can provide information tailored to the specific needs of groups and individual users in your organization. Easily create reports using the familiar Excel front-end then automatically schedule and distri- bute them to any number of recipients according to your Qlik® authorization strategy. Information can be prepared as a visual or interactive analysis AS WELL as in standard report formats including PDF, Excel or HTML. Even employees without an interactive Qlik® Sense Dashboard can access and view your results.

WITH SENSE EXCEL REPORTING YOU CAN:

  • Create and Distribute Reports On-Demand using Microsoft® Excel as your authoring tool
  • Automate your reporting, scheduling and distribution efforts
  • Re-use your Qlik® Sense Role-based security attributes
  • Receive imports utilizing Qlik ETL capabilities
  • Create, Distribute and Dynamically Name Reports based on Conditions
  • Utilize a Loop and Reduce Strategy to Report on an Automated Series
  • Flexibly deploy using distribution strategies including via Qlik® sense HUB, E-mail Attachments, folders and/or network locations

Seamless integration between Microsoft® Excel and your Qlik® Sense BI Platform
